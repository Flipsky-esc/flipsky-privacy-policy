Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This application is developed and provided by Flipsky ("we", "us"). Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

In certain features, the app may request access to the following information:

  • Bluetooth Permission: Used to communicate with your devices (such as ESCs). We do not collect or upload any Bluetooth communication data.
  • Location Information: Location permission is required only to enable Bluetooth connections, which is a technical requirement on iOS. We do not store or share any geographic location data.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable app functionality such as communicating with Bluetooth devices.
  • To improve app performance and identify/resolve bugs.

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal regulations;
  • To protect our legitimate rights.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use SDKs provided by Apple or other services for Bluetooth communication, performance analysis, etc. These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Flipsky ESC Tool

The mobile version of Flipsky ESC Tool uses the device’s location services in both foreground and background. When using the real-time logging feature, location access is required to scan Bluetooth devices and record position data alongside motor data. Background location access is needed in common use cases such as putting the device in your pocket while continuing to record.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our services may contain links to other websites operated by us. If you click such links, you will be directed to that site, which may provide firmware downloads and our sales channels.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
